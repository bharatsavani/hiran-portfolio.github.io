Yet another article on Bullet Journaling.

For the last three and a half years, I am into Bullet Journaling. It’s a framework for analog journaling, created by Ryder Carroll. It’s not like a planner you get from the store that forces you to follow specific formats. It’s an extensible and customisable system that gives a variety of ways to use it . And there are thousands of “BuJo tips and tricks and templates” available online.
